Kakashi Hatake – The Copy Ninja



Kakashi Hatake is a legendary ninja from Konohagakure (Hidden Leaf Village), known as the Copy Ninja due to his ability to copy over a thousand jutsu with his Sharingan. He is the leader of Team 7, consisting of Naruto Uzumaki, Sasuke Uchiha, and Sakura Haruno. Later, he becomes the Sixth Hokage of the Hidden Leaf Village.
He is famous for his laid-back attitude, intelligence, and mastery of various jutsu. Despite his calm and sometimes lazy demeanor, Kakashi is one of the strongest ninjas in the series.

Sasuke Uchiha – The Last Uchiha
Sasuke Uchiha is one of the last surviving members of the Uchiha Clan and a major character in Naruto. He is introduced as Naruto Uzumaki’s rival and teammate in Team 7 but later leaves the village to seek power. His life is driven by revenge against his older brother, Itachi Uchiha, who massacred their clan. Over time, Sasuke’s journey leads him from vengeance to redemption, becoming one of the strongest shinobi in history.

Sakura Haruno – The Strongest Kunoichi
Sakura Haruno is a skilled medical ninja and a member of Team 7, alongside Naruto Uzumaki and Sasuke Uchiha, under Kakashi Hatake’s leadership. Initially, she is known for her crush on Sasuke and her rivalry with Ino Yamanaka, but she later develops into one of the strongest kunoichi (female ninja) in history. Trained by Tsunade, one of the Legendary Sannin, Sakura becomes a powerful healer and fighter, playing a crucial role in the Fourth Great Ninja War.

"Naruto" is a Japanese manga and anime series created by Masashi Kishimoto. It follows the journey of Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja who dreams of becoming the Hokage, the strongest ninja and leader of his village, Konohagakure (Hidden Leaf Village).

1. Basic Information
Masashi Kishimoto
Manga: Published from 1999 to 2014 in Weekly Shōnen Jump
Anime:Aired from 2002 to 2017 (with a sequel, Boruto)
Genres: Action, Adventure, Fantasy, Martial Arts.
2. Story Overview
The story is divided into two parts:
A. Naruto (Part 1) – Follows Naruto as a young ninja trying to gain recognition.
B. Naruto: Shippuden (Part 2) –
Follows an older Naruto facing stronger enemies and deeper mysteries.Naruto starts as an outcast because he has the Nine-Tailed Fox (Kurama) sealed inside him. As he trains and fights, he makes friends and uncovers secrets about his past, his parents, and the village.
He is the main protagonist, orphaned at birth. Holds the Nine-Tails (Kurama) inside him. Learns Shadow Clone Jutsu and Rasengan. Becomes Seventh Hokage in Boruto.
3. Major Characters 
Sasuke Uchiha : Naruto’s rival and best friend.Last surviving Uchiha after Itachi kills the clan.Seeks revenge on his brother and later fights Naruto.Awakens Sharingan, later the Mangekyō Sharingan and Rinnegan.

Sakura Haruno : Naruto and Sasuke’s teammate.Trains under Tsunade and becomes a top medical ninja.Loves Sasuke but initially rejects Naruto.

Kakashi Hatake : Team 7’s mentor, known as "Copy Ninja Kakashi."Has Sharingan from his late friend Obito Uchiha.Becomes the Sixth Hokage after the Fourth Great Ninja War.

Madara Uchiha : One of the strongest Uchiha, main villain in later arcs.

Obito Uchiha : Initially thought dead, becomes Tobi and leads the Akatsuki.

Itachi Uchiha : Sasuke’s brother, misunderstood hero.
